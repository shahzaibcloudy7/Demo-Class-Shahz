BISMILLAH - "In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful" (In Arabic: بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ, In English: bi-smi llāhi r-raḥmāni r-raḥīmi)

PEACE BE UPON YOU ALL (In Arabic): ٱلسَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ, (In English): as-salāmu ʿalaykum)

This is my first page on "The Shahada (Arabic: ٱلشَّهَادَةُ aš-šahādah - "the testimony")

لا إله إلا الله محمد رسول الله (in Arabic) (lā ʾilāha ʾillallāh, Muḥammad rasūlu-llāh) (in English) It reads: "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is his messenger."" (in English)

The testimonies: The declaration reads: لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّٰهُ lā ʾilāha ʾillā -llāhu There is no deity but God. مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ muḥammadur rasūlu -llāh Muhammad is the messenger of God. The above two statements are commonly prefaced by the phrase ašhadu ʾal ("I bear witness that"), yielding the full form: أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّٰهُ وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ ašhadu ʾalā ʾilāha ʾilla -llāhu, wa-ʾašhadu ʾanna muḥammadan rasūlu -llāh. It reads: I bear witness that there is no deity but God, and I bear witness that Muhammad is the messenger of God.
