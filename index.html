BISMILLAH - This is my first page on "The Shahada (Arabic: ٱلشَّهَادَةُ aš-šahādah - "the testimony")

"In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful" (Arabic: بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ, English: bi-smi llāhi r-raḥmāni r-raḥīmi)

PEACE BE UPON YOU ALL (Arabic: ٱلسَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ, English: as-salāmu ʿalaykum)

لا إله إلا الله محمد رسول الله (lā ʾilāha ʾillallāh, Muḥammad rasūlu-llāh) (in Arabic) There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is his messenger. ( in English)

It reads: "I bear witness that there is no deity but God, and I bear witness that Muhammad is the messenger of God."
